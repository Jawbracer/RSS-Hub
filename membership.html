Membership

How to join.

Membership in the party is secured by joining the Discord server and showing us that you align with our core values and political goals. All requisite information can be found on the server. If you join you are expected to follow the rules of the server first, and then the rules set by the party.

What is expected of you.

There are no fees, no strings attached, you participate for whatever reason you want and for as much time as you can spare. There are no commitments that will result in you being removed if you do not follow through. Lorvale Republic is primarily for fun, to build a growing community of polically minded people, as an outlet for them to express their core values to one another.

Consider Joining us on Discord.
