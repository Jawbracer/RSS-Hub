Unity In Solidarity

What We Stand For

Information is Freedom.

We believe that free and open access to information is key to a functioning democracy. All of our members are reguarly encouraged to read, learn and in turn educate others. Information is nothing without context, raw information can be dangerous and misleading, we believe in giving you that context, to help you understand the world around you better than before.

Marx and you.

Karl Marx is a figure best known for his economic philosophy and his criticism of the prevailing economic systems that exitsed then and even still today. Whilst we do not follow any specific tendacy as a Party, the criticisms that Marx levied at the world around him still ring true to many of our members.

Democracy today.

We believe that democracy lays at the heart of any modern socialist movement. Democracy can only fully deliver on its promises when those within society are given the freedom to express their will. Chief among these freedoms is economic freedom, freedom from poverty, from class stuggle, from oppression.

Building something for all of us.

We recognise that the world is never as we want it to be, that something can always be improved for the better. That is why we look to both the future and the past to inform our policy positions, to guide our judgement, and to help the people we care about the most.
